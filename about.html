My name is Charles Graham and I am a Web Developer. I have been working in the industry for a short time just after I finished my bootcamp. I am currently working in my Web development bootcamp to become a much more knowledgeable coder in the tech world. I have worked in construction for the past 10 years or so mostly operating heavy machinery. My last job was working on a 200 ton crane in San Diego, CA. I have been wanting to change into a career in tech for a while and was convinced by my fiancee that it was about time I did so.

When i finally started making the move towards my goal to be a web developer, It was one of the anxiety riddled moves I had made considering I had been in a steady job for years but just was not enjoying it as I once did. The first few months were pretty tough and I tried to learn a lot on my own through sites such as w3schools.com and developer.mozilla.org and these helped to try and get me where I was going but still didnt know all the basics and needed a little help here and there.
